How The Board Operates
You submit a Board Packet — your decision question, the context, the constraints, and the stakes.
1 The Chair confirms the question and convenes the Board.
2 The Truth Round — each seat diagnoses through their lens. No solutions yet.
3 Proposals & Cross-Examination — each seat proposes, then collision begins.
4 Motions, Votes & The Seal — formal motions, individual votes, and a sealed Decision Record.
Consensus is the enemy of clarity. The Board earns its keep through collision.
State your case.
The Board requires context before it convenes.
