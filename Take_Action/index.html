Help Us Bear This

You sat in the chair. Now stand up and act.

You have just come from something heavy. If your hands are shaking, that is the right response. If you feel sick, that is the body recognizing what the mind already knows. If you are angry—good. Anger is the immune system of a republic.

You did not read every file. You did not have to. You sat in the room with what they contain. The Committee of Patriots walked you through evidence that three million pages of court records, flight logs, sworn depositions, and authenticated correspondence have made undeniable. You know what you know now. And you cannot unknow it.

This is not a petition. This is not a form letter. This is a personally addressed demand from a constituent to the people who swore an oath to protect you and your children. The tool below writes that letter in your voice, to your representatives, and puts it in their hands.

The children in those files did not have a voice. You do. What you do with it in the next three minutes will be counted.

Your Voice
This is the part that makes your letter different from every other message in their inbox. You are not a lobbyist. You are a human being who just sat with the evidence. Tell them what that did to you.

When you read what was done to those children—when you saw the names and the flight logs and the silence of the powerful—something happened inside you. Name it. You do not have to explain it. Just speak it.

Delivery
