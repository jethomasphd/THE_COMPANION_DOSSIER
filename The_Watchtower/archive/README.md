This is a frozen snapshot of the **original four-doctrine Watchtower**, preserved unedited.

- **S&P 500 (SPY)** — the passive benchmark
- **Wells Fargo DSIP** — the Diversified Stock Income Plan *(retired in Vol. II)*
- **UBS Aggregate** — four UBS funds blended into a single line *(disaggregated in Vol. II)*

breakout, the March war, the April ceasefire, and the late-May mega-cap rally —

on rate-cut multiple expansion.

The live Watchtower has since been **reconstituted** as the Second Vigil:

(DVRUX, QGRPX, Mid Caps USA, BNIEX), staged against the Republic and SPY.
