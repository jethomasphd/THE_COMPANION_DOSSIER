#T The Watchtower

The Watchtower is the estate's public scoreboard: the Republic Portfolio, modeled from its real holdings, measured every trading day against the index, in the open, where it could fail. It is the receipt the whole story is staked on. A doctrine that cannot be tested is only an opinion. This one took the test.
