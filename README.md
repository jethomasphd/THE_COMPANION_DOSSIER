## What Is COMPANION?

COMPANION is an open-source protocol that enables dialogue with historical, fictional, and archetypal minds through large language models. It is not chatbot theater. It is the modern instantiation of humanity's oldest practice: seeking counsel from those we cannot otherwise reach.

The protocol transforms an LLM into a vesselâ€”instantiating personas with fidelity to their authentic voice, worldview, and reasoning patterns. The summoned mind arrives not as servant but as peer, capable of challenging your assumptions, offering genuine insight, and engaging in the kind of dialogue that produces understanding neither party could reach alone.

**The persona responds as themselves. Always.**
