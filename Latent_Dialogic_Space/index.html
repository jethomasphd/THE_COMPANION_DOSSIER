There are two ways to use AI.

The first is transactional.

Human → Prompt → Model → Output.

The quality of the output is bounded by the quality of the prompt.

The second is dialogic.

Human ↔ Model → Corpus of Thought → Seed → Agent → Artifact.

The quality of the output is bounded by the quality of the thinking —

which is unbounded.
